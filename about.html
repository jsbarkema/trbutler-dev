About Thomas R. Butler Inc.

Since 1967, Thomas R. Butler, Inc., has been a leader in the building of some of the finest developments in California's San Joaquin Valley. But ours is more than just a building company - it's a family tradition in quality, innovation and craftsmanship. Founded by Thomas R. Butler, past president of the San Joaquin Valley Building Industry Association, and his wife, Carole, the company also benefits from the day-to-day involvement of sons Scott, Greg and Stephen Butler. It all comes together to give Thomas R. Butler, Inc., a well-deserved reputation for building homes and commercial properties with consistently thoughtful designs, strategic locations and detailed craftsmanship. Join the family tradition that ensures superior housing, rental and commercial values, backed by old fashioned pride, integrity and quality workmanship.
